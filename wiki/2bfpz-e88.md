# FCC ID 2BFPZ-E88 - E88 Quadcopter Filing

## Summary

This is the FCC ID database record for the E88 quadcopter (FCC ID: 2BFPZ-E88), submitted by Shantou Chenghai District Donghang Toy Factory and granted on April 17, 2024. The filing covers a 2.4 GHz low-power transmitter classified under FCC Part 15C, used for both RC control and FPV WiFi video transmission.

The product is marketed commercially as a 1080p/4K HD FPV WiFi camera drone with carrying bag. The FCC record is the primary source of verified radio and regulatory technical data; camera and flight performance claims come from retail listings and are unverified by this filing.

The exhibits include user manual, internal and external photos, RF exposure info, test reports, and - filed under long-term confidentiality - schematic and block diagram. The confidential exhibits are not publicly accessible via fccid.io.

## Key Takeaways

- The E88 operates exclusively in the 2420-2460 MHz band (a 40 MHz slice of the 2.4 GHz ISM band), certified under FCC Rule Part 15C (spread spectrum / digitally modulated unlicensed transmitter above 1 GHz)
- Both RC control and FPV video use the same 2.4 GHz WiFi radio - the drone acts as a WiFi access point and the pilot's phone or remote connects to it
- Schematic and block diagram are under long-term FCC confidentiality; internal photos and test reports are publicly accessible
